"100% NATURAL AWARD WINNING SOAPS"

We are a small factory located in Lyre,
A small village in the northern county Cork, Ireland,
approximately 3km from the village of Nad.

Our soap making uses a cold process by mixing
fatty acids and sodium hydroxide (oil & lye) which
then triggers the saponification process.

We carry 14 different award winning soaps
of of them are 100% natural

The lyre soap company are quickly becoming industry leaders
